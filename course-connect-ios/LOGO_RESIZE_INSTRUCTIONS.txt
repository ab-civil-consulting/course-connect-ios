========================================
HOW TO RESIZE APPLE TV ICONS
========================================

Recommended Approach:
1. Use an image editor (Photoshop, GIMP, Figma, etc.)
2. Open course-connect-logo-blue.png (or white for top shelf banners)
3. Create new canvas with target dimensions
4. Center logo on canvas with padding/margins
5. Export as PNG with transparent background

Tips for Apple TV Icons:
- Icon files (icon_*, iconSmall*): Use blue logo centered on transparent background
- Top Shelf files (topShelf*): Use WHITE logo on DARK or transparent background
  (Apple TV UI is dark, so white logo shows better)
- Maintain aspect ratio of logo, add padding to fill rectangular dimensions
- Keep logo crisp - don't stretch to fill entire space

Online Tools:
- https://www.iloveimg.com/resize-image (PNG resizing)
- Figma/Canva (free design tools with precise canvas sizing)

Command Line (if you have ImageMagick):
# Resize maintaining aspect ratio, center on canvas
magick course-connect-logo-blue.png -resize 1000x600 -background transparent -gravity center -extent 1280x768 icon_1280x768.png
